Salmon with Lemon and Dill


This recipe is a delightful take on a flavorful fillet of salmon. Bold flavors like lemon and dill help to give the fish a lovely taste, while foil helps to make for easy cleanup


Ingredients:

  • One fillet of salmon
  • One lemon, sliced
  • One sprig of dill, pulled apart into small pieces
  • One teaspoon of butter, cut into thin slices
  • Aluminum foil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 420 degrees
  2. On a baking dish, lay enough aluminum foil to accommodate the salmon fillet
  3. Lay the salmon fillet on top of the foil, skin down
  4. Lightly salt the salmon fillet
  5. Arrange sliced butter on top of the salmon
  6. Arrange sprigs of dill on top of the butter, covering the entire salmon
  7. Arrange slices of lemon on top of the butter, covering the entire salmon
  8. Cook the salmon for 20 to 25 minutes at 450 degrees, varying time for weight
  9. Salmon is done after slicing the fillet in half and verifying the center is no longer uncooked pink
  10. Before serving, remove the lemon and dill
